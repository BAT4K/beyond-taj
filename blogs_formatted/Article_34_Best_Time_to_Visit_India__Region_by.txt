# Best Time to Visit India — Region by Region Guide

**Meta description:** India has no single "best time." Here's the month-by-month, region-by-region breakdown so you can pick the right window for your specific trip.

**Target keyword:** best time to visit india

---

"October to March" is the standard answer and it's incomplete. India is a subcontinent — the best time depends entirely on where you're going.

## The Quick Answer

| Region | Best Months | Avoid |
|---|---|---|
| North India (Delhi, Agra, Jaipur, Varanasi) | Oct-Mar | May-Jun (45°C+) |
| Rajasthan | Oct-Mar | May-Jun (extreme heat) |
| Kerala | Nov-Feb | Jun-Jul (monsoon, unless doing Ayurveda) |
| Goa | Nov-Feb | Jun-Sep (monsoon, everything closes) |
| Himalayas (Shimla, Manali, Dharamsala) | Mar-Jun, Sep-Nov | Dec-Feb (snow, road closures) |
| Ladakh | Jun-Sep ONLY | Oct-May (passes closed, flights unreliable) |
| Northeast India | Oct-Apr | Jun-Sep (heavy monsoon) |
| Tamil Nadu & Pondicherry | Nov-Feb | Oct-Nov (northeast monsoon) |
| Andaman Islands | Nov-Apr | Jun-Sep (rough seas, limited ferries) |

## Month-by-Month Summary

**October:** Post-monsoon, green landscapes, lower prices, fewer crowds. Our top pick for value.

**November:** Excellent everywhere except Tamil Nadu (northeast monsoon). Diwali falls here some years — book 6 months ahead.

**December-January:** Peak season. Best weather across North and South India. Highest prices, most crowded. Book everything early.

**February:** Excellent across India. Holi (late Feb/early Mar) adds festive energy.

**March:** Warming up in the north. Still good for South India. Ladakh still closed.

**April-May:** North India gets brutal (40-45°C in Delhi/Rajasthan). Hill stations are perfect. Ladakh starts opening.

**June-September:** Monsoon across most of India. Ladakh is open and stunning. Kerala Ayurveda treatments are traditionally done during monsoon (humidity helps). Everywhere else — challenging for tourism.

## The Festivals Factor

| Festival | 2026 Date | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Diwali | November 8 | Hotels in Rajasthan, Varanasi booked months ahead |
| Holi | March 4 | North India celebrations, read our Holi safety guide |
| Pushkar Fair | Nov 17-24 | Pushkar hotels sell out 6 months ahead |
| Christmas/New Year | Dec 25-Jan 1 | Goa and Kerala peak pricing |
| Navratri/Dussehra | Oct | Rajasthan celebrations, good time to visit |

## The Contrarian Picks

- **Monsoon Kerala (Jun-Aug):** Lush, uncrowded, cheapest rates. Ayurveda is traditionally prescribed during monsoon.
- **October anywhere:** Post-monsoon, everything green, pre-peak pricing. Best value month.
- **April Himalayan hill stations:** While Delhi melts, Shimla/Manali/McLeod Ganj are perfect 20-25°C.

## Takeaway

There is no single "best time." October-March works for most routes. Ladakh is summer only. Hill stations are best in spring. Monsoon Kerala is an underrated gem. Plan by region, not by country.
