# Best India SIM Card for Foreign Travelers (2026)

**Meta description:** Which SIM to buy, where to buy it, what documents you need, how long activation takes, and the eSIM alternative.

**Target keyword:** best india sim card foreign travelers

---

A working Indian phone number is essential — for Uber/Ola, WhatsApp, Google Maps, and emergency contacts. Here's how to get one.

## The Two Best Options

**Jio** — Best coverage nationwide, fastest data, cheapest plans. ₹459 for 28 days with 2GB/day data + unlimited calls.

**Airtel** — Second-best coverage, slightly better in some rural/mountain areas. ₹499 for 28 days with 2GB/day data + unlimited calls.

Both work well. If you're going to remote areas (Ladakh, Northeast India, rural Rajasthan), check coverage maps — Jio is generally better in cities, Airtel slightly better in some remote areas.

## Where to Buy

**Best:** Official Jio or Airtel counters inside international airport terminals (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Kochi). Available 24/7 at Delhi T3.

**Avoid:** Unofficial kiosks or touts outside airports — they charge 3-5x the price.

## What You Need

- Passport (original)
- Passport-size photo (carry 2-3 from home)
- Indian contact number (your hotel's number works)
- Payment in cash (₹500-700)

## Activation Time

- Airport counters: usually activated within 2-4 hours
- Some SIMs take up to 24 hours for full activation (calls work immediately, data may take longer)
- **Tip:** Buy the SIM immediately upon landing while you're at the airport. By the time you reach your hotel, it should be active.

## The eSIM Alternative

If your phone supports eSIM (most iPhones since XS, most Samsung since S20):
- **Airalo** — buy an India eSIM online before flying. Data only, no Indian phone number. Starts at $5 for 1GB.
- **Holafly** — unlimited data India eSIM. $19 for 5 days, $47 for 15 days.

**Limitation:** eSIMs provide data but no Indian phone number. You'll need WhatsApp for calls. Some services (OTP verification, Uber registration) may require a real Indian number.

**Best combo:** eSIM for data on landing + physical Jio/Airtel SIM for phone number.

## Takeaway

Buy Jio or Airtel at the official airport counter on arrival. Bring passport + photo. ₹500-700 gets you 28 days of data + calls. Consider an eSIM for backup data from the moment you land.
