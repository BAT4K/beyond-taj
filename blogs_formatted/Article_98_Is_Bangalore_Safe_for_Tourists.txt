# Is Bangalore Safe for Tourists?

**Meta description:** Bangalore (Bengaluru) is one of the safest major cities in India for foreign tourists.

 Why Bangalore Is Safe

- Tech hub mentality — cosmopolitan, educated, tolerant
- Strong nightlife culture — women go out safely at night in areas like Indiranagar, Koramangala, MG Road
- Good public transport (Metro expanding rapidly)
- Lower tout/scam density than tourist cities (Bangalore isn't a "tourist city" — it's a living city)
- Uber/Ola work excellently

 Best Areas

- Indiranagar: Restaurants, bars, walkable streets. Safe at night.
- Koramangala: Cafés, co-working, startup culture.
- MG Road / Brigade Road: Shopping, dining, central.
- Whitefield: Tech corridor, modern, clean.

 What to Do (As a Tourist)

Bangalore isn't a monument city — it's an experience city.

---

* **Best for:** food scene (India's best craft beer scene, incredible South Indian food), Bangalore Palace, Cubbon Park, Lalbagh Botanical Garden, day trips to Mysore (3 hours) and Nandi Hills.

## Takeaway

Bangalore is safe, modern, and welcoming.

## The Verdict

Best as a stopover or digital nomad base, not a primary tourist destination. Excellent food, great nightlife, safe for solo women.
