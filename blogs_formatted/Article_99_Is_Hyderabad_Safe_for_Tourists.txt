# Is Hyderabad Safe for Tourists?

**Meta description:** Hyderabad is safe, under-visited, and has some of India's best food. Here's the honest guide for foreign visitors.

**Target keyword:** is hyderabad safe tourists

---

Hyderabad is one of India's most under-visited major cities by foreign tourists — and one of its safest.

## Safety Level: Safe

- Modern city with good infrastructure
- Tech industry presence (Microsoft, Google, Amazon campuses)
- Friendly locals accustomed to international visitors
- Uber/Ola work well
- Lower tout density than North Indian tourist cities

## What to See

- **Charminar:** Iconic 16th-century monument. Old city around it is chaotic but fascinating.
- **Golconda Fort:** Massive, well-preserved fort with acoustic engineering and sunset views.
- **Taj Falaknuma Palace:** Former Nizam's palace, now a luxury hotel. High tea here is one of India's most remarkable experiences (₹2,500/person — worth it).
- **Ramoji Film City:** World's largest film studio complex. Fun for families.
- **Salar Jung Museum:** One of India's best collections. Free on Sundays.

## The Food

Hyderabadi biryani is India's most famous dish. Eat it at: Paradise, Bawarchi, Shah Ghouse, or Café Bahar. ₹200-400 for a plate that could feed two.

## Takeaway

Hyderabad is safe, affordable, culturally rich, and has India's best food. 2-3 days is ideal. Under-visited by foreign tourists — which means fewer touts and more authentic interactions.
