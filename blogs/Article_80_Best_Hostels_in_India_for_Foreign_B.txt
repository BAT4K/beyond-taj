# Best Hostels in India for Foreign Backpackers (2026)

**Meta description:** The hostel chains that work, city-by-city recommendations, what to look for, female-only dorms, and the ones to avoid.

**Target keyword:** best hostels india backpackers

---

India's hostel scene has matured significantly. Here are the chains and individual hostels that consistently work for foreign backpackers.

## The Big Three Chains

**Zostel** — India's largest hostel chain. 40+ locations nationwide. Clean, social, good common areas. Female-only dorms available. ₹400-800/dorm bed, ₹1,500-3,000/private room. Consistently well-reviewed by foreign travelers.

**Moustache Hostel** — 15+ locations. Strong in Rajasthan (Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, Jaisalmer). Good rooftop common areas. ₹350-700/dorm bed. Popular with European backpackers.

**GoStops** — 20+ locations. Clean, modern, good WiFi. ₹400-900/dorm bed. Slightly more upscale than Zostel. Party-friendly at some locations.

## What to Check Before Booking

- **Lockers:** Essential. Confirm they're large enough for a backpack and have your own padlock.
- **Female-only dorms:** Available at all major chains. Specify when booking.
- **Hot water:** Confirm — not guaranteed at budget hostels.
- **WiFi:** Available at all chain hostels but speed varies. Don't plan to do video calls from hostel WiFi.
- **Reviews from foreigners:** Filter Hostelworld and Booking.com reviews by foreign travelers — they flag different issues than Indian reviewers.
- **Common area:** Hostels with rooftops or gardens have better social scenes than corridor-only hostels.

## What to Avoid

- **Unnamed guesthouses marketed as hostels** in Paharganj, Varanasi old city, and Old Delhi — these are often budget hotels with shared rooms, not proper hostel infrastructure
- **Party hostels if you need sleep** — some GoStops and Zostel locations in Goa are social until 2 a.m.
- **Hostels without reviews from solo women** if you're a solo woman

## The Hostel vs. Hotel Decision

| Choose Hostel | Choose Hotel |
|---|---|
| Solo traveler wanting to meet people | Couple wanting privacy |
| Budget under ₹1,000/night | Budget over ₹2,000/night |
| Cities on the backpacker trail | Off-trail small towns |
| Staying 1-2 nights | Staying 3+ nights (private room becomes better value) |

## Takeaway

Zostel, Moustache, and GoStops are reliable across India. Book through Hostelworld or the chain's own website. Confirm lockers, female dorms, and hot water. Avoid unnamed guesthouses calling themselves hostels.
