# Is Udaipur Safe for Solo Female Travelers?

**Target keyword:** is udaipur safe solo female travelers

---

One of India's safest cities for solo women. Lake-side, walkable old city, heritage hotels, tourism-dependent economy that values visitor safety. Touts are present but less aggressive than Jaipur or Agra. Safe to walk alone during daytime in the old city. Use Uber/Ola for evening transport. Avoid isolated lakeside paths after dark. The overall atmosphere is romantic, calm, and photographer-friendly. BeyondTaj rates Udaipur as the #1 safe city for solo women in North India.
