# India for Couples — Romance, Safety, and PDA

**Meta description:** India is excellent for couples but PDA rules are different. Holding hands = fine.

**Target keyword:** india travel couples guide

---

Kissing in public = uncomfortable for locals (not illegal, but culturally awkward). Heritage hotels in Rajasthan are among the world's most romantic stays.


* **Best couple destinations:** Udaipur, Kerala backwaters, Goa, Andaman Islands, Jaisalmer desert camps.

Book private experiences (boat dinners, rooftop yoga, cooking classes). Avoid Varanasi and Delhi as "romantic" destinations — incredible, but intense. BeyondTaj couple itineraries balance romance with adventure — palace hotels plus real India.
