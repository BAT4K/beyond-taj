# Ayurveda in Kerala — The Honest Guide

**Target keyword:** ayurveda kerala foreigners guide

---

Legitimate Ayurveda in Kerala requires 7-21 day programs with BAMS-qualified doctor consultation, personalized treatment, and Kerala Tourism-certified facilities (Green Leaf or Olive Leaf certification). Cost: ₹5,000-15,000/day. A 60-minute spa "Ayurvedic massage" is not Ayurveda. Recommended: Kottakkal Arya Vaidya Sala (most traditional), CGH Earth's Kalari Kovilakom (luxury), Somatheeram (government-certified, budget-friendly). Monsoon (Jun-Aug) is traditionally the best time — humidity helps oils penetrate. BeyondTaj connects travelers with certified Ayurvedic centers, not tourist-trap spas.
