## Rules for All Three Routes

- **Maximum 3 nights in one place** for the first half (keep moving while energy is high)
- **Minimum 3 nights** in the second half (slow down as fatigue hits)
- **One recovery day built in** around day 5-6
- **Fly between distant legs** — Delhi-Varanasi, Chennai-Delhi. Don't waste 12 hours on a train when a 2-hour flight costs $40.
- **Book accommodation for the first 3 nights before flying.** Leave the rest flexible.
