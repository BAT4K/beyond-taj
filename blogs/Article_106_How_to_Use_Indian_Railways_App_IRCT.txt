# How to Use Indian Railways App (IRCTC)

**Meta description:** Download IRCTC Rail Connect app. Register with passport number + international phone (OTP may require the app, not browser).

**Target keyword:** how to use irctc app foreigner

---

Search trains by route and date.


* **Look for seat availability:** "Available" = confirmed, "RAC" = you'll get a berth but may share, "WL" = waitlisted (risky — may not confirm).

Book AC 2-Tier for comfort. Pay by international card. Check PNR status in-app.

## The Verdict

Track train location with "Where is my Train" feature. Foreign Tourist Quota must be booked at station counters, not online.
