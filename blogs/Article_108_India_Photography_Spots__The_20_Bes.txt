# India Photography Spots — The 20 Best Shots

**Target keyword:** best photography spots india tourists

---

Top shots by region. **Delhi:** Humayun's Tomb at sunrise, Lodhi Art District murals. **Agra:** Taj from Mehtab Bagh at sunset, Taj reflection pool at sunrise. **Jaipur:** Hawa Mahal from street level, Panna Meena ka Kund stepwell. **Udaipur:** City Palace from Lake Pichola boat, old city rooftop sunset. **Varanasi:** Sunrise boat ride on Ganges, Manikarnika Ghat smoke at dawn (from distance, no close-up). **Kerala:** Backwater houseboat at sunset, Munnar tea plantations in mist. **Ladakh:** Pangong Lake, Thiksey Monastery at sunrise. **Rajasthan:** Jaisalmer Fort golden hour, Pushkar Lake at dusk. All locations best at 6-7 a.m. or 5-6 p.m. for golden light. BeyondTaj includes golden-hour timing for every site on every itinerary.
