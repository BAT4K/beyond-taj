# Best Travel Apps for India — The 12 You Actually Need

**Meta description:** The specific apps that work in India for transport, maps, translation, food, and emergencies. Download before you fly.

**Target keyword:** best travel apps india foreigners

---

Download these before landing. They work, they're free, and they solve real problems.

## Transport
- **Uber** — Cars and autos in all major cities. International cards accepted.
- **Ola** — India's Uber equivalent. Sometimes better availability. Cash or Indian card.
- **IRCTC Rail Connect** — Official Indian Railways booking app. Train schedules, PNR status, seat availability.
- **redBus** — Bus booking for intercity travel. Volvo sleeper buses bookable with ratings.
- **MakeMyTrip** — Flights, hotels, buses. Good for last-minute domestic flights.

## Navigation
- **Google Maps** — Works well in cities. Less reliable in rural areas and old-city lanes. Download offline maps for your route.
- **Maps.me** — Better offline maps than Google for remote areas (Ladakh, Northeast, rural Rajasthan).

## Communication
- **WhatsApp** — India's primary communication tool. Hotels, drivers, tour operators all use it. Install before arriving.
- **Google Translate** — Download Hindi, Tamil, and Bengali language packs for offline use. Camera translation works on signs and menus.

## Food & Health
- **Practo** — Telemedicine app. Doctor consultations in English. Prescription delivery. ₹300-500/consultation.
- **Zomato** — Restaurant discovery, delivery, and reviews. Useful for finding safe restaurants near your hotel.

## Emergency
- **112 India** — Official emergency app. SOS button, location sharing. Works like 911.

## Pro Tips
- Download all offline content (maps, translation packs) before leaving your hotel each morning — mobile data can be patchy in rural areas
- WhatsApp > calling for most Indian communications — it's free over data and everyone uses it
- IRCTC app for real-time train tracking ("Where is my Train" feature)
- Zomato ratings are reliable — 4.0+ is consistently good

## Takeaway

12 apps. Download before landing. The ones you'll use daily: WhatsApp, Uber/Ola, Google Maps, Google Translate. The ones you'll be glad you have when you need them: Practo, 112 India, IRCTC.
