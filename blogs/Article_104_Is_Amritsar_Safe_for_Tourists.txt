# Is Amritsar Safe for Tourists?

**Target keyword:** is amritsar safe tourists

---

Very safe. The Golden Temple area is one of the most secure tourist zones in India — constant security, CCTV, and community policing. The Wagah Border ceremony is well-organized with designated foreign visitor seating. Amritsar's Punjabi culture is warm, food-obsessed, and welcoming. Stay near the Golden Temple (Heritage Street area). 1-2 nights is ideal. Pair with McLeod Ganj (4 hours) for a 5-day Punjab-Himachal itinerary.
