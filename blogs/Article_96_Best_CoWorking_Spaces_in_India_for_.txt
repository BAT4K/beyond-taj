India's co-working scene has exploded. Here are the best options by city.

**Day passes:** ₹300-800 at most spaces. Try before committing monthly.

**Pro tip:** Your Jio/Airtel mobile hotspot (20-50 Mbps) works as reliable backup. Co-working spaces are best for video calls and heavy uploads.

## Takeaway

Goa and Bangalore are the strongest nomad bases. Pondicherry and Udaipur are budget gems. Day passes let you test before committing. Mobile hotspot is your reliable backup everywhere.
