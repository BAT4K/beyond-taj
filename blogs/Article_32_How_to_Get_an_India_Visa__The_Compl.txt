# How to Get an India Visa — The Complete 2026 Guide for Tourists

**Meta description:** e-Visa types, fees by nationality, the application process step by step, common rejection reasons, and the mistakes that delay your visa.

India's e-Visa system is straightforward once you know the options. Here's the complete process as of 2026.

## The Three Tourist Visa Options

| Visa Type | Duration | Fee (most nationalities) | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| 30-day e-Tourist | 30 days, double entry | $25 (Jul-Mar) / $10 (Apr-Jun) | Short trips, first-timers |
| 1-year e-Tourist | 1 year, multiple entry | $40 | Returning visitors, long trips |
| 5-year e-Tourist | 5 years, multiple entry | $80 | Frequent visitors |

**Country-specific fees:** US citizens pay $25-160 depending on duration. UK citizens pay up to $484 for 5-year. Japanese/Sri Lankan citizens pay $25 flat. Check the official fee schedule at indianvisaonline.gov.in.

## Step-by-Step Application

1. **Go to indianvisaonline.gov.in** — this is the ONLY official portal. Avoid third-party sites that charge extra "processing fees."

2. **Select "e-Tourist Visa"** and fill in personal details, passport information, and travel plans.

3. **Upload documents:**
   - Passport photo (white background, 2"x2", JPEG, 10KB-1MB)
   - Passport bio page scan (JPEG/PDF, 10KB-300KB)

4. **Pay online** — credit/debit card or PayPal. Additional 2.5-4% bank transaction charge applies.

5. **Wait 2-5 business days.** Peak season (Oct-Mar) can take longer. Apply at least 2 weeks before travel.

6. **Print the ETA (Electronic Travel Authorization)** — carry a printed copy. You'll need it at immigration.

## Common Mistakes That Cause Delays or Rejection

- **Photo doesn't meet specs.** White background, no glasses, face clearly visible. This is the #1 rejection reason.
- **Passport has less than 6 months validity.** Must have 6+ months from date of arrival AND 2 blank pages.
- **Applying on a fake portal.** Only use indianvisaonline.gov.in. Sites like "india-evisa.it.com" or "e-visa-india.com" are third parties that charge $50-100 extra.
- **Applying too late.** Minimum 4 days before travel required. Recommend 2-3 weeks.
- **Wrong visa type.** Conference, business, and medical visas are separate categories — tourist visa doesn't cover working or volunteering.

## Entry Points

The e-Visa is accepted at 33 airports, 19 seaports, and 4 land border crossings (as of 2026). All major airports (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Kochi, Goa) are covered.

## At Immigration

- Have your printed ETA ready
- Have your passport ready
- Have your hotel booking confirmation ready (they sometimes ask)
- Have a return/onward flight ticket (they sometimes ask)
- Biometrics (fingerprints) are taken at immigration — this is normal

## Visa on Arrival?

India does NOT have a general visa on arrival for most nationalities. The e-Visa must be obtained before travel. Exception: Japanese and South Korean citizens can get visa on arrival at select airports.

## Takeaway

Apply at the official portal, use a compliant photo, apply 2+ weeks early, and print your ETA. The process takes under 30 minutes online.
