Indian trains are transportation and an experience. These 8 routes are worth taking.

## The Must-Do Routes

**1. Gatimaan Express: Delhi → Agra** (1.5 hours)
India's fastest train. AC chair car, meals served. ₹750-1,000. The most efficient way to reach the Taj.

**2. Shatabdi Express: Delhi → Jaipur** (4.5 hours)
AC day train, meals included. ₹700-900. Comfortable, reliable, scenic.

**3. Vande Bharat Express: Various Routes**
India's newest semi-high-speed trains. Modern, clean, fast. Routes include Delhi-Varanasi, Delhi-Jaipur, Chennai-Mysore. ₹800-2,000.

**4. Konkan Railway: Mumbai → Goa** (10-12 hours)
92 tunnels, stunning Western Ghats scenery, bridges over rivers. One of India's most scenic rail journeys. Book AC 2-Tier for comfort.

**5. Nilgiri Mountain Railway: Mettupalayam → Ooty** (5 hours)
UNESCO World Heritage toy train. Steam locomotive climbs through tea plantations. ₹300-600. Book months ahead — sells out.

**6. Kalka-Shimla Railway** (5-6 hours)
UNESCO heritage narrow-gauge line. 102 tunnels, 800+ bridges. Stunning Himalayan scenery.

**7. Darjeeling Himalayan Railway** (7-8 hours)
The original "toy train." Steam engine through tea gardens with Kangchenjunga views. UNESCO World Heritage.

**8. Rajdhani Express: Delhi → Mumbai or Delhi → Kolkata** (16-18 hours)
India's premier long-distance train. AC, all meals included, sleeper berths. The quintessential Indian train experience.

## Booking Tips

- Book 60-90 days in advance for popular routes
- Use IRCTC app or irctc.co.in
- Foreign Tourist Quota available at railway station counters with passport
- AC 2-Tier (2A) is the comfort sweet spot
- Heritage trains (Nilgiri, Kalka-Shimla, Darjeeling) sell out months ahead — book earliest possible

## Takeaway

Don't skip Indian trains. The Konkan Railway, heritage mountain railways, and Vande Bharat Express are experiences in themselves. Book early, choose AC 2-Tier, and use the Foreign Tourist Quota.
